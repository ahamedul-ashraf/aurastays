Who We Are

Curating the World's Most Exquisite Stays

Founded in 2018, Aura Stays was established on a single principle: travel should be a transformative art. We set out to build a platform that strips away the noise, offering discerning travelers direct access to the world's most spectacular sanctuaries.

Our select listings aren't just hotels; they are architectural wonders, historic mansions, wellness sanctuaries, and private islands where every detail is tailored to provide a sense of place and quiet luxury.

Our Vision

To redefine high-end hospitality curation by creating a global community where architecture, wellness, and bespoke services unite, offering an unmatched standard of luxury travel.

Our Mission

To match discerning travelers with handpicked premium properties, ensuring a flawless booking workflow, personalized itinerary concierge services, and maximum security.
