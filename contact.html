Frequently Asked

What is the standard cancellation window?
Most of our property listings offer free cancellation up to 48 hours prior to check-in. Please review individual room policies during room selection.
How do I request early check-in?
You can add early check-in requests directly into the checkout page "Special Requests" input. Our butler concierge will coordinate with the hotel and notify you.
Can I pay using cryptocurrencies?
Currently, we support major Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and select digital wallets. Cryptocurrency billing pipelines are planned for next year.
