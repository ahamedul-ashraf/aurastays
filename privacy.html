Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you share directly with us while setting up accounts, completing checkouts, or submitting message forms. This data includes your full name, email, phone numbers, billing address details, and custom travel requests.

2. How We Use Information

We process details specifically to execute billing transactions, issue PDF receipts, provide customer support channels, coordinate with luxury hotel partner portals, and distribute optional promo newsletter packages.

3. Data Protection Security

We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption, firewalls, and tokenized payment architectures to ensure your details are protected. Credit card verification data is never persisted directly in our databases.

4. Cookie Allocations

Aura Stays uses necessary system cookies to preserve your layout theme configurations (Dark vs. Light mode), check search session parameters, and verify login authentication credentials.

5. Your Legal Rights

You retain full access to review, correct, adjust, or request deletion of your personal database records. To submit a request, contact us via support email details listed on the contact page.
