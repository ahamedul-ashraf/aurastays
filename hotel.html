Aman Sanctuary

Aman Resorts, Tokyo

Otemachi Tower, Chiyoda City, Tokyo, 100-0004, Japan

Sanctuary Description

Occupying the top six floors of the prestigious Otemachi Tower, Aman Tokyo is a monument to modern luxury, blending traditional Japanese style with contemporary grandeur. High ceilings, shoji screens, and large washi paper lanterns characterize the property, providing an atmosphere of absolute tranquility amidst the bustle of Japan's capital.

Our guests enjoy world-class hospitality features, including a monumental lobby looking onto the Imperial Palace gardens, an 80-foot temperature-controlled infinity pool, and the multi-award-winning Aman Spa offering traditional Japanese Kampo wellness therapies.
